Meet Laraib: Botanical Researcher

Specializing in plant nanotechnology and sustainable agriculture

About Laraib

Laraib is a passionate botanist who graduated with honors in 2024. Her research focuses on the intersection of nanotechnology and plant biology, with a special interest in sustainable agricultural practices.

During her academic journey, Laraib developed innovative approaches to enhance plant resilience and nutrient uptake using nanotechnology, particularly in beetroot cultivation.

Research Focus

Nanoparticles in Beetroot

Her groundbreaking thesis explored how zinc oxide (ZnO) nanoparticles affect pigment concentration and growth rates in Beta vulgaris (beetroot).

Sustainable Agriculture

Developing nano-fertilizers to enhance plant growth and improve crop yields under salt stress conditions.

Plant Stress Responses

Investigating how nanoparticles can help plants withstand environmental stressors.
